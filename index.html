About Us

Feasible and Innovative solutions are presented to the Management, and implemented.

Hack4Amrita is a initiative by members of FOSS@Amrita club with a goal to solve real-world problems faced by Amritians, by innovating feasiable solutons through Hackathons.

How it works?

We are gonna follow three steps.

Raising an Issue

Students/Faculty (or other stakeholders) in the campus raise an issue they are facing.

Solving Them

Developers of FOSS@Amrita (and other interested developers) team up for an Hackathon, and try solving the issues.

Implementaion Time!

Feasible and Innovative solutions are presented to the Management, and implemented.
